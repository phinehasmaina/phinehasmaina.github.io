Soccer is my favorite sport! Whenever I am free I usually play soccer with friends. I competed in this year's college intramural soccer and was able to qualify for the playoffs with my team. My favorite soccer club in Manchester United situated in England.

I got exposed to programming when in 2020 through some python classes I took during my gap year before college. These classes played a huge factor in me choosing my major since I originally did not really know what I wanted to do. Learning python made me love programming and I have not looked back since.
